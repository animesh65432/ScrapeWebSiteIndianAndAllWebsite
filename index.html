Chief Minister Leads Samvidhan Diwas Observance at Manan Kendra

Date: 26-Nov-2025
Gangtok, November 26: The state observed Samvidhan Diwas today with Chief Minister Mr Prem Singh Tamang leading the celebrations and calling for a deeper commitment to constitutional values. Joining the people of the state in honoring the Constitution of India, the Chief Minister highlighted that the nation’s guiding document stands on the pillars of justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity.
As part of the program held at Manan Kendra, the Chief Minister led the reading of the Preamble and participated in activities aimed at strengthening public understanding of the Constitution and its ideals.
Addressing the gathering, the Chief Minister urged citizens especially the youth to uphold constitutional principles in their daily lives and contribute to building a stronger and more inclusive society. He further highlighted the need to renew the collective resolve to follow the vision of Dr. B.R. Ambedkar and the framers of the Constitution, as the state continues to work toward progress and national development.
